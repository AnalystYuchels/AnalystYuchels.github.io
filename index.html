About

A bit about me

I'm Uchechi Uche, a full-stack web developer and technical writer based in Lagos, Nigeria. I am currently building my skills through project-based learning; I learn by building real, deployable applications rather than just following tutorials.

My background in technical support means I approach code the way I approach problems: methodically, with an interest in understanding the why before fixing the what. I believe good software and good good documentation are two sides of the same coin.

When I'm not writing code or documentation, I'm usually reading (fiction and non-fiction), learning something new, thinking about the next project to build, or simply just watching a movie.
